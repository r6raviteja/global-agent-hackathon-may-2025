### About Freed
- **Company Background:** Freed addresses clinician overwork, burnout, and high turnover.
- **Mission:** Use AI and strong execution to create products that improve clinician happiness.
- **Achievements:** 
  - Acquired 10,000+ clinicians in 1 year.
  - Exceeded $11M ARR.
  - Generate 70,000 patient notes daily; over 1 million monthly.
  - Made thousands of clinicians happier.
- **Funding & Growth:** Backed by Sequoia; expanding product offerings including patient-facing assistants, insights, and EHR integrations used by thousands of clinicians and millions of patients.

---

### About the Role
- **Position:** Data Analyst in the Product team.
- **Goal:** Support a data-driven culture, enable better decision-making.
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Work with product managers, engineers, data analysts, and stakeholders.
  - Uncover and deliver insights.
  - Build dashboards and track metrics.
  - Design experiments and analyze results.
  - Understand user context and team goals.
  - Contribute to improving clinician happiness.

---

### Impact & Responsibilities
- Guide teams to make data-informed decisions.
- Create metrics, dashboards, and experiments to understand product and clinician behaviors.
- Collaborate with stakeholders for analysis and insights.
- Work with engineers to improve data models, transparency, and accessibility.

---

### Requirements
- 2-4 years of experience in data analytics or related field.
- Proficiency in SQL and data visualization tools.
- Comfort with scripting languages (Python preferred, R acceptable).
- Strong understanding of experiment design and A/B testing.
- Excellent collaboration and communication skills.
- Ability to work independently, prioritize, handle high ambiguity.
- Deep curiosity, attention to detail, proactive attitude.
